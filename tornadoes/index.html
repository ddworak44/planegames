Tornado Game

Build tornadoes with ASCII characters and battle your opponent!

Player 1 Controls (Left Tornado):

  • A - Spin left (build tornado, move left)
  • D - Spin right (build tornado, move right)
  • W - Shoot projectile at opponent's highest tier
  • S - Duck (remove top layer to avoid attacks)

Player 2 Controls (Right Tornado):

  • ← → - Arrow Left/Right: Spin left/right
  • ↑ - Arrow Up: Shoot projectile
  • ↓ - Arrow Down: Duck (remove top layer)

Game Rules:

  • Build your tornado by spinning (A/D or Arrow keys)
  • Shoot at your opponent's highest tier
  • Duck to avoid shots, but be careful!
  • If you're at level 0 (base level) and get hit, you're eliminated!
  • Maximum 10 levels per tornado

Strategy: Build up layers, shoot strategically, and duck at the right moment!
