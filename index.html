Constellation Game
How to Play: Click to place stars on the night sky. Use arrow keys to connect stars, creating beautiful constellations. The arrow keys will connect the current star to its nearest neighbor. Press "Save as PNG" to save your creation.
Tornado Game
How to Play: A two-player competitive game! Build tornadoes with ASCII characters and battle your opponent.
Player 1: A/D keys spin left/right (build tornado), W shoots, S ducks (removes top layer).
Player 2: Arrow Left/Right spin, Arrow Up shoots, Arrow Down ducks.
Shoot at your opponent's highest tier! Duck to avoid attacks, but be careful - if you're at level 0 and get hit, you're eliminated!
